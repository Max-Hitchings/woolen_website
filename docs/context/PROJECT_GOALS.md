# Project Goals

## Primary Goal
Launch a premium-feeling Woolen website that makes the brand appear established, explains the value of the sauna hat clearly, and converts visitor interest into waitlist signups.

## Current Phase
Pre-launch brand and conversion buildout. The site already exists in a polished form, but key launch-readiness items are still being turned from placeholders into production-ready systems.

## Immediate Priorities
- Finalize the waitlist flow with a real persistence or email platform integration.
- Maintain premium brand consistency across homepage and supporting content pages.
- Improve trust and launch readiness with stronger social proof, operational detail, and production content where placeholders still exist.
- Keep the mobile experience polished and performant.

## Secondary Priorities
- Expand educational and brand storytelling content without diluting the restrained tone.
- Improve technical robustness around form handling and operational setup.
- Preserve a clean architecture so future product or content additions remain easy to implement.

## Non-Goals
- Building a full ecommerce checkout flow right now
- Expanding into a broad multi-product catalog before the first product story is solid
- Introducing flashy animations, loud visual treatments, or trend-driven wellness messaging
- Performing refactors that do not improve launch readiness, maintainability, or user experience

## Success Signals
- Visitors can clearly understand what Woolen is and why the sauna hat matters.
- The site feels premium, cohesive, and mobile-first.
- Waitlist submissions can be reliably captured in a production system.
- Agents and collaborators can understand the repo quickly from the shared docs and make changes without re-learning project intent each time.
